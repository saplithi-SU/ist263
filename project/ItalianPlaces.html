Italian Restaurant Recomendations

Tarallucci E Vino

This restaurant's motto is "all ends well with Tarallucci E Vino." It perfectly represents this fantastic Italian restaurant's experience with numerous locations all around in NYC, including the Upper Westside, Nomad, Union Square, and the East Village. This place has been serving real authentic Italian food since 2001. My go-to meal at this place is the burrata and Spaghetti Pomodoro.

Serafina

Serafina is easily one of the most famous Italian restaurants in the city! With over 10 locations in the city, this restaurant proves to be one of the best. Its mission since 1991 is to provide customers with the highest quality food in a welcoming atmosphere. My recommendation is to go to the Serafina in the meatpacking district and order the Margherita pizza and the buffalo mozzarella tomato and basil.

John's on 12th

If you are looking for a traditional old Italian restaurant, then John's on 12th is the perfect place for you! This restaurant is notorious in NYC since it's been around since 1908 in the east village. But it has adapted to fit the time era we are currently in by offering many vegan and dairy free meals. My mom and I' go-to order is eggplant parmigiana with vegan cheese.
